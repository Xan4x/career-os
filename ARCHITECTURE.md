# Architecture

## Purpose

Career OS is a knowledge base.

It is the single source of truth describing my professional career.

Generated documents (CV, LinkedIn, portfolio, biographies...) are outputs.

Career OS itself is never generated.

---

# Principles

- Single Source of Truth
- Single Responsibility
- Knowledge before presentation
- Reusable information
- No duplication

---

# Information Flow

person
        \
companies \
customers  ---> projects ---> domains ---> deliverables
        /
notes  /

Deliverables are generated from Career OS.

Career OS is always updated first.
